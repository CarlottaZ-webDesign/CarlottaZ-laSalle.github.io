Whispers of Twilight

Zandali Carlotta

Beneath the sky of dusky hue,
Whispers of twilight softly brew.
Stars emerge in a cosmic ballet,
Painting dreams on the canvas of night's array.

Moonlight weaves its silver song,
As shadows dance,graceful and strong.
Night unfolds its mystic veil,
A celestial story, both ancient and frail.

In the quietitude, the world takes flight,
Nocturnal symphony, pure delight.
Silent echoes in the tranquil air,
A celestial embrace, beyonf compare.
